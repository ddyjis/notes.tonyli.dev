# Tesla

## Differences between Tesla and other car manufacturers

- Recycle plan for materials
- Plan to own mineral mines
- Low production cost (only 3 parts for car frame)
- No marketing
	- Elon Musk claimed they put the money in R&D
	- Elon Must himself is the marketing
- B2C directly, no dealer in-between
- Got 30% gross profit (around 2022) vs Toyota (~15%)
- Has plan for future economy model
	- e.g. FSD to earn money when idle
