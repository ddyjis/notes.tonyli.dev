# Canadian Depositary Receipts

Issued by CIBC and listed on NEO exchange. CDRs allow Canadians to access selected foreign (mainly
US for now) stocks in CAD.

[List of available CDRs](https://www.neo.inc/en/services/raising-assets/canadian-depositary-receipts)

As of 2022, CDRs are new and lightly traded, meaning there is liquidity risk. CAD hedged could be an
issue / at least some risk #todo: more on this

It would be interesting if there are stocks from other countries (e.g. Samsung from South Korea /
TSMC from Taiwan)
