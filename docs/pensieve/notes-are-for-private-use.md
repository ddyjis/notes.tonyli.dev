# Notes are meant for private use

Pensieve is my tools to assist my thinking. Its main goal is to serve myself. So audience other than me do not have a role here.

Andy Matuschak once said in his notes

> ... If a note seems confusing or under-explained, it’s probably because I didn’t write it for you! Sorry—that’s sort of an essential tension of this experiment (Write notes for yourself by default, disregarding audience).

Also note that Pensieve in Harry Potter are considered private. This is also only of the reason why I choose the name my personal knowledge system "Pensieve".
