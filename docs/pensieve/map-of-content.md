# Map of Content

I used map of content wrong before. I link a note to MOC notes and in the MOC notes, I rely on the
backlinks to get back the notes. Although I call them "Map of Content", they are simply tags.

A real MOC should be using forward links which are part of a passage in the MOC note. It should aid
the smooth flow of thoughts and lead me to the notes I need.

Just like writing a note, developing a MOC note from stretch is stupid and not practical. Below is a
comment from Obsidian forum saying that MOC should grow out from tags.

> One recommendation I’ve seen in a few places is to start with tags and only add a MOC once a
> particular tag area becomes large enough to warrant the extra effort of creating and maintaining a
> MOC.  
> (forgot to copy the source URL. This comes from a comment on Obsidian forum)
