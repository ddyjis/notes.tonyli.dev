This Docusaurus is my personal "Pensieve". I use it to store my notes and organize my mind.

The name "Pensieve" comes from Harry Potter which is formed by combining `pensive` and `sieve`. In the story, the owner of a pensieve stores memories in it. By reviewing the memories, the owner can re-organize their thoughts and make decisions. And this is what I do here - store and review ideas and notes, making the Pensieve a GTD and PKM system. And at the end bring positive impact to my life. (Does it?)

I set up a few instructions for myself to follow in order to allow the organic growth of the ideas.
