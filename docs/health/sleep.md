# Sleep

## Problem with lack of Sleep

- Weakened immune system
  - 4hr/night then 12hr/night => 50% decrease in antibodies for a flu vaccine
- Higher risk of heart disease
- Lower sex drive
- Raise risk of weight gain
- Raise risk of diabetes
